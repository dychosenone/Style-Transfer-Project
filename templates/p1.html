Image Style Transfer

The main inspiration for this program comes from Leon Gatys in his 2016 paper titled “Image Style Transfer Using Convolutional Neural Networks”. The style transfer site allows users are able to upload photos of different kinds to the site to see what the photo would look like with the style of another photo. Digital Style Transfer is a technique wherein photos of a said content could be remade (or "styled") into a photo with a said style. Developed by Lorenz Alog, Jacob Dy, Jose Noblefranca and Patrick Ong for the DIGIMAP course.
